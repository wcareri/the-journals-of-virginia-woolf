### Friday 23 March

No, not much reason to complain. L. has just come in with an offer from Maynard of the literary editorship of the Nation. Well, thats unexpected! Here have I been toiling these 3 weeks to make Eliot take it; finally he shied; & this is the result. No doubt there are drawbacks, but it means safety for the moment, indeed luxury.
